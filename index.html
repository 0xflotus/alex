Introduction

Whether your own or someone else’s writing, alex helps you find gender favouring, polarising, binary thinking, identity insensitive, race related, religion inconsiderate, or other unequal phrasing.

For example, when We’ve confirmed his identity is given to alex, it will warn you and suggest using their instead of his.
